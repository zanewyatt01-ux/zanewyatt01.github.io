A Beginner's Guide to Baseball



Baseball is an amazing sport if you know how to play it and is open to people of all ages. On this website, I will show you the fundamental aspects of baseball and how to get started in your baseball journey.



What is the goal of Baseball?



The main goal of baseball is to score more runs than the opposing team through either fielding the ball and getting players out by beating them to the base with the ball or throwing the ball to the basemen OR hitting the ball with a bat and running and touching around the bases, eventually making it to home base and scoring a run for your team. The game is played in usually a period of 8 or more innings (depending on the score) and each team will spend one half of the inning either batting or fielding the ball.
